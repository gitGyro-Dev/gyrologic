# Gyro Logic – Categorical Formulation

---

## Categories

- 𝒮 : Structure category  
- 𝒳 : Observation category  

---

## Functor (Observation)

O : 𝒮 → 𝒳  

---

## Morphisms (Transitions)

f : S₁ → S₂  

---

## Diagram

D : I → 𝒮  

---

## Identity

I = lim ← O(D)

Identity is the limit of observed structures.

---

## Stability

Stab_O(S) =
∫ κ(O(S), O(S')) dμ(S')

---

## Interpretation

- Structure → objects  
- Transition → morphisms  
- Observation → functor  
- Identity → limit  
- Stability → measure  

---

## Summary

Gyro Logic provides:

- identity as categorical limit  
- observation as functor  
- stability as measure-based robustness
